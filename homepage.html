How to Donate Blood?

You may please visit a Clinical Laboratory center and identify your blood group. The clinical test will cost you 50-60 INR only. The result shall be provided in 2 hours time. In case you wish to donate blood now itself, visit your nearest Blood Collection Center. Or alternatively you may register as donor in Indian Blood Bank. Recipients or their relatives or Hospital authorities will contact you directly.To know the benefits of blood donation..click here

Donor Guideline

Each second of every day, someone needs blood. You can make the choice to donate by being a volunteer donor to save one's life. Never will there be a substitution for your donation. It takes less than an hour to make a life-giving blood donation. To save life, both donor and recipient safety has to be considered.
